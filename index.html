Ghost Diary – Data Deletion Request

At Ghost Diary, we value your privacy and your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

How to Delete Your Account

  • In-App: You can permanently delete your account and cloud data via the "Delete Account" option in the Profile settings within the app.
  • Via Email: Alternatively, send an email to serverappsofficial@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Once your request is processed:

  • Your email address and authentication profile.
  • All your private diary entries.
  • All your "Whispers" (public posts).
  • Any metadata associated with your account.

Note: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
